This is the landing page for the 2025 Lunar Gala [LG] Creative Archive. The archive acts as an index for future generations of LG, preserving creative assets that didn't make it to the final show.

Recognizing that Lunar New Year is celebrated across many cultures, we turned the landing page into an open call, inviting Pan-Asian creators to design their own versions and share their interpretations of the holiday — the essence of LG.
